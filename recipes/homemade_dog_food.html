• 5 pounds choice of meat
  • 1 pound carrots
  • 2 pounds brocolli
  • 2 pounds cauliflower
  • 3 cups brown rice
  1. Begin cooking meat in pan over medium heat
  2. while meat is cooking finely chop vegetables andbegin cooking rice
  3. when meat is nearly fully cooked add veggies to pot or pan
  4. let vook for around 10 minutes stirring often
  5. remove from heat and add rice, mix well
  6. portion into containers and store in fridge or freezer
