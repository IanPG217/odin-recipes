Simple Mac and Cheese

The simplest of mac and cheeses, no roux, no cream, no complexity. this is the storebought ramen of mac and cheeses, if there wasn't already mac and cheese you could buy at the store.

Ingredients

  • noodles of choice
  • cheese or cheeses of chioce

Instructions

  1. cook noodles per instructions
  2. drain noodles then move pan back to heat
  3. add cheese to pan, add as much or as little as you want
  4. stir frequently and serve once cheese is melted
